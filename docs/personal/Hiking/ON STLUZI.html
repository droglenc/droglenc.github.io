St. Luzikapelle and Mittenberg Loop

Chur, Switzerland

On our second full day in Switzerland (October 6, 2024) we had made our way to the historic city of Chur, the oldest city in Switzerland. While we did city walks (I, II) the next day, we were itching to get into the countryside. The trail we decided on started on the edge of town fairly near our hotel and quickly climbed via several switchbacks through beautiful woods to St. Luzikapelle, a pulchritudinous little chapel in a rock grotto. From there we continued to climb until we reached an alpine meadow near Mittenberg. This area screamed “Sound of Music”, and the overlooks to the north and west were stunning. From there was a nice descent (with lots of switchbacks) back to Chur. This hike was a great introduction to the Swiss landscape, we are glad we did it.
