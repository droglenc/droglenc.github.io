Cedar Valley Nature Trail

County Home Rd to W Main St

While home for a family reunion, Kim, Cash, my sister Pam, and I walked the Cedar Valley Nature Trail from County Home Rd to W Main St near Cedar Rapids. The trail was flat, paved, and straight primarily going through ag land and private houses with a wooded buffer along the trail. It was a nice day to “get out.”
