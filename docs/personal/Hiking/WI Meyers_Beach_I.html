Meyers Beach Lake Shore Trail

Cornucopia, WI

One of our favorite hikes is the Lake Shore Trail at Meyers Beach in the Apostle Islands National Lakeshore. While often quite busy with other hikers it is still a nice hike above the sea caves along the Lake Superior shore. The views of The Crevasse, pillars, and The Bowl are always great.
