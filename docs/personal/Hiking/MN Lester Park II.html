Lester Park Loop II

Amity East and Lester River Trails

On December 28, 2023, Kim, Cash, and I made a nice loop hike in Lester Park. We went “out” on the Amity East trail and came back “in” on the Lester River trail. This was a very nice long(ish) hike in Lester Park. We did not take any pictures.
