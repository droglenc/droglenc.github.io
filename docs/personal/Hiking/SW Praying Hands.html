Praying Hands Loop

from First Water Road

On February 8, 2025 Kim and I hiked past the Praying Hands Rock via several trails, including the Praying Hands trail, off the First Water Road. The route was fairly flat along Jacob’s Crosscut Trail but began to climb while on Prospector’s View, Treasure Loop, and the start of Praying Hands trails. At the saddle near Praying Hands Rock we detoured to take a “social trail” behind a cut rock, which eventually required quite a bit of scrambling in loose rock (on some fairly steep areas) before reconnecting with the Praying Hands Trail. That sidetrack was fun, but probably not needed! The Praying Hands Trail back to the parking lot was a fairly easy gradual descent. Fun trail. Would do it again.
