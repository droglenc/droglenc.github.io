Tanawha Trail - Beacon Heights to Rough Ridge

Blue Ridge Parkway, NC

On October 6, 2025, Kim and I hiked with my nephew Dillon on the Tanawha Trail from the Beacon Heights parking area to the Rough Ridge Overlook; thus connecting to where we left off the day before. This was a winding, rough (from boulders) at times, beautiful trail through some nice outcrops with amazing views of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Its always fun to hike with Dillon in his adopted home state!
