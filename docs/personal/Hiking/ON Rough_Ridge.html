Tanawha Trail - Rough Ridge

Blue Ridge Parkway, NC

On October 5, 2025 and on our way from Charlotte to Boone (and following our Linville Gorge hike), Kim and I hiked the Tanawha Trail along the Blue Ridge Parkway from the Rough Ridge parking area to the Rough Ridge overlook. This was a nice short trail with several amazing overlooks of the Blue Ridge Mountains.
