Bald Cypress Trail

Lost Landing SP, Virginia Beach

On February 27, Kim and I hiked the Bald Cypress Trail in Lost Landing State Park in Virginia Breach with her niece Journey. This was a fun little hike through the Bald Cypress swamps with a short detour out to see one of the inland waterways (Long Creek). It was a beautiful day and it was great to spend time with Journey before her championship meet.
