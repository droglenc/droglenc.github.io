Wave Cave

Lost Goldmine & Carney Springs Trails

On March 6, 2022 Kim and I hiked from the first Peralta Trailhead parking area to the “Wave Cave” via the Lost Goldmine, Carney Springs, and Wave Cave trails. The trail was fairly flat until the Wave Cave trail portion, which ascended quickly. The actual cave was larger than I expected and offered up some great photos. There were a decent number of people on the trail on this beautiful day. Details are below.
