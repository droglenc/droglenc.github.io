Rim Trail

Grand Canyon National Park, AZ

The Rim Trail is a flat, mostly paved trail that runs for approximately 13 miles along the South Rim of the Grand Canyon in Grand Canyon National Park. The trail is an easy walk and provide a great and beautiful overview of the canyon. We actually walked from Mather Point to Bright Angel Trailhead on the first afternoon we arrived at the canyon and then walked from South Kaibab Trailhead to Mather Point after hiking the South Kaibab trail the next day. The track below puts these two walks together as if they were one walk.
