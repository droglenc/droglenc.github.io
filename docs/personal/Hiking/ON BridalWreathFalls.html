Bridal Wreath Falls to Garwood Trail Loop

Saugoro National Park, Tucson, AZ

On February 12, 2025 Kim and I hiked the Douglas Springs Trail out to Bridal Wreath Falls. There was no water at the falls, so it was not as impressive as we had hoped (but it was not a surprise that it was dry). On the way back we took Three Tank Trail to Carillo Trail and Garwood Trail. This was a much more interesting hike with LOTS of Saguaro cacti, including the famous cristate Saguaro on the Garwood Trail.
