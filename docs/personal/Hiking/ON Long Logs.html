Long Logs and Agate House Trails

Petrified Forest National Park, AZ

After the Crystal Forest Trail we stopped at the Long Logs and Agate House Trails. The first part of the Long Logs Trail was on an old road. We turned off this to follow the Agate House Trail to the Agate house, which was both interesting and beautiful (see the pictures below). The rest of the Long Logs trail was neat, with many large petrified wood specimens.
