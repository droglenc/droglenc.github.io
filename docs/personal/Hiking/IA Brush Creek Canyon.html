Brush Creek Canyon State Preserve

User-Defined Loop

Cash and I walked the road into and the trail that starts at the parking area of Brush Creek Canyon State Preserve. The trail eventually disappears but I made a loop below the bluffs to where two streams come together before eventually ending on top of the bluffs for a hike back to the parking area. This was a very beautiful hike through a mature hardwood forest with a main stream and several feeder streams as well as a number of impressive limestone bluffs. The view from the “overlook” was very nice.
