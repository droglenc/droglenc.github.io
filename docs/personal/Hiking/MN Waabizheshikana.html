Waabizheshikana Trail Loop

Duluth, MN

Kim, Cash and I went to Duluth on April 2, 2024 to get “away” for a good meal and beer. As part of the day we walked the Waabizheshikana Trail in Duluth, MN, with a loop back along a few city roads. It was an interesting trail mostly through residential areas, with a few industrial areas, along the river. The weather was a little rainy and snowy, so we did not take any pictures, but we still had a good time.
