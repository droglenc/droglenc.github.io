Virginia Beach Boardwalk

On February 27-29, Kim and I stayed in a hotel right on the Virginia Beach boardwalk. Each day we walked all (or most) of the boardwalk with good weather on most days, and a variety of people to watch! On the second to last day, Kim spotted dolphins just off-shore!!
