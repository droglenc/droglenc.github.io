Northern Great Lakes Visitor Center

Ashland, WI

Cash and I walked the trails at the Northern Great Lakes Visitor Center near Ashland on 3-Feb-25. We started in the Aldo Leopold parking lot, looped around before crossing over to the visitor center, and the looped around the boardwalks and wetlands near the visitor center before returning to the parking lot. This is a nice short hike.
