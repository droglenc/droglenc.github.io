Makwa Ziibiins Miikana Trail

Superior, WI

On April 26, 2022 Cash and I hiked the Makwa Ziibiins Miikana Trail in Superior, WI. This is a fun little trail from the edge of town out to wetlands on Allouez Bay. When we were there they were just starting to build a floating boardwalk through the wetlands to connect with Wisconsin Point. My understanding is that it is now complete. We will have to do this trail again.
