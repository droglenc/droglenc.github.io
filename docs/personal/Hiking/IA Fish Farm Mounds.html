Fish Farm Mounds State Preserve

User-Defined Loop I

Cash and I explored Fish Farm Mounds State Preserve and adjoining Fish Farm Mounds Wildlife Management Area on November 26, 2024. It is always humbling and inspiring to be near the burial sites of Indigenous peoples, thought the story (here and here) of how the mounds have been “researched” (i.e., pillaged) is, while predictable, still disheartening. The trails behind the mound complex area were well maintained (i.e., cleared) and interesting as they traversed through mature hardwoods, rocky outcrops, and deep ravines.
