Erwin’s View Trail

Linville Gorge, Linville Falls, NC

On October 5, 2025, Kim and I hiked Erwin’s View trail in the Linville Gorge to see Linville Falls up-close and from a distance at two beautiful overlooks. This was a busy but beautiful trail and crews were working hard to get back in shape after Hurricane Helene knocked down many trees a year earlier.
