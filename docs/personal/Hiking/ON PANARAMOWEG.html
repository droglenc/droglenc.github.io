Panaramoweg Kleine-Scheidegg to Mannlichen

Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland

Kim and I took the train from Lauterbrunnen to Kleine Scheidegg to walk the Panaramoweg trail to Mannlichen, including the Royal Walk, and back. This is one of the highest regarded trails in Switzerland because the trail is releatively flat and easy to walk while affording amazing views of several mountain peaks. Unfortunately, we did not have any clear days in Lauterbrunnen so we did not get to see the peaks. However, it was still a fun hike through the fog and snow. I guess we will have to return some day for the full experience.
