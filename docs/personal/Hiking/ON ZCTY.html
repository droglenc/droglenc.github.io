City Walk

Zurich, Switzerland

We landed in Switzerland on October 5, 2024 and took the train to the city center for a “get over jet lag” night. To get out and stretch our legs we took a meandering route through town to see the sights. Zurich is a big city and felt as such, but it was a fun walk.
