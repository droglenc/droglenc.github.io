Chur City Walk II

Chur, Switzerland

On our second day in Chur (October 7, 2024) we made a couple of walks around town to get a better feel for this old city. This earlier city walk was largely around the main city. This second walk started at our hotel, wound through town to the outskirts for a steep ascent through the woods before we were stopped by trail construction. We then back tracked back to town and wound around various parts of the city before getting back to our hotel.
