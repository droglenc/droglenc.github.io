Temperance River Loop

Temperance River State Park

On January 12, 2024, Kim, Cash and I hiked along the Temperance River (in the Temperance River State Park) from the parking area on Highway 61. The beginning portion of the trail along the river is gorgeous with several falls and swirling plunge pools. Parts of the trail are right on the edge of the river. Upstream from all of the falls is still beautiful, just not as awe inspiring. Eventually we turned away from the river and generally walked south once the [Superior Hiking Trail]((https://superiorhiking.org/) turned north towards Carlton Peak. This portion of the trail was a very nice walk through the woods, though we were laboring some in the snow (we could have used our snow shoes). Eventually we returned to the SHT along the river where we turned back towards the parking lot. We had a very nice hike!
