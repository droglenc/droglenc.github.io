Painted Desert Rim Trail

Petrified Forest National Park, AZ

After a quick stop at the visitor center to Petrified Forest National Park we embarked on the short Painted Desert Rim Trail. The panoramic views were impressive and it was fun to see the Painted Desert Inn. Our next stop was the Puerco Pueblo Trail.
