South Crest Trail, Sandia Peak

Albuquerque, NM

On February 20, 2023 Kim and I hiked part of the South Crest Trail on Sandia Peak after taking the Sandia Peak Aerial Tram up the mountain. There was a lot of snow and we were not fully prepared for the much colder temperatures so we did not hike the complete trail. However, the portion that we did was beautiful and provided some amazing view “over the edge.”
