City and Musegg Wall Walk

Lucerne, Switzerland

Also, on October 12, we explored the Musegg Wall and some of its nine towers. We left our hotel and meandered through town to reach the western gate at Schirmerturm, then explored several of the towers before exiting at Nölliturm to meander through town back to our hotel. Exploring the towers was pretty cool and offered amazing views of Lucerne.
