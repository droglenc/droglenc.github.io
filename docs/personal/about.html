About

I very much enjoy spending time with my wife Kim and our sheltie Cash (and previous shelties Jem and Jordan). In addition, I enjoy tending to our property on the Muskeg Creek; fishing, boating, and underwater photography in the summer; and cross-country skiing and snowshoeing in the winter. I also enjoy listening to music and podcasts.

Since getting an iPhone and Cash, I have also done a lot of hiking/walking followed by mapping our hikes/walks.

Not surprisingly, I also make occasional TV appearances; the most famous of these is my appearance on Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives.
